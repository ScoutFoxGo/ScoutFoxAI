# ScoutFoxGo — Business Model, Roadmap & Partners

## Development roadmap
- **Phase 1 — Web app launch + beta testing:** get the web app live, test with real
  families, prove it saves time and that people want it.
- **Phase 2 — Marketing + partnerships:** grow awareness; build relationships with
  tourism boards, attractions, municipalities, parks, schools, libraries, family
  brands, and local businesses.
- **Phase 3 — Mobile app:** build iOS and Android after web validation (est.
  $40,000–$60,000).

## Beta program
A "Founding Families" beta, then expansion beyond families (accessibility, special
needs, young professionals, business travelers, single adults, baby boomers).
Phase 1 web beta → mobile beta.

## Revenue opportunities
Multiple potential streams:
- Premium subscriptions / family planning memberships
- Sponsored placements and featured local attractions
- Tourism partnerships and destination marketing
- Affiliate links and brand sponsorships
- School / community tools

**Long-term opportunity:** become the trusted platform families use when deciding
what to do, where to go, and how to spend meaningful time together.

## Potential partners
Organizations that want to reach families at the moment they are deciding what to do:
- Tourism boards and destination marketing organizations
- Museums, zoos, parks, libraries, cultural institutions
- Municipalities, chambers of commerce, economic development orgs
- Schools, camps, enrichment programs, family organizations
- Family entertainment centers, restaurants, hotels, event organizers
- Family-focused brands and technology partners

For partners, Scout creates value by helping their activities and destinations reach
the **right families at the right time**.

## Funding snapshot
Founder-funded (~$20,000 invested to date). Estimated next raise $75,000–$150,000 to
fund beta testing ($5–15k), marketing/user growth ($15–50k), partnership development
($5–15k), working capital ($10–25k), and future mobile development ($40–60k).
Explored Kickstarter (later canceled to pivot), YC and Next Wave applications, SBA
financing, and angel outreach.

## Two related products
- **Scout Fox Go™** — the consumer family decision-intelligence platform.
- **ScoutFoxAI** — a separate but related AI platform (multi-model AI comparison and
  synthesis), with Free / Pro / Team pricing concepts. It is the AI brain/backend
  that can stand alone and later connect to Scout Fox Go.
