# ScoutFoxGo — Company & Product Overview

## What ScoutFoxGo is
Scout Fox Go™ is an **AI-powered decision intelligence platform** that helps
families and busy individuals spend less time researching and more time confidently
discovering, planning, and enjoying local activities, travel experiences, and
real-world adventures.

- **Tagline:** Plan Less. Explore More.
- **Core concept:** a decision engine (not a chat box) that helps people answer one
  question — *"What should we do today?"* — by narrowing options to what actually
  fits their family, budget, location, schedule, and preferences.
- **Company:** Scout Fox Go, LLC — a woman-owned Florida LLC. Founder/CEO: Meghan.
- **Mascot:** Scout Fox. **Brand colors:** teal, blue, orange, green.

## The problem Scout solves
Families have more information than ever, but planning got *harder*, not easier. A
parent planning a simple weekend outing may need to check Google, social media,
review sites, maps, weather, event calendars, prices, drive time, food options, age
suitability, parking, safety, and family preferences. The issue isn't too few
options — it's **too many disconnected options and not enough help deciding.**

## The solution
Scout acts like an **intelligent planning assistant**. It helps users discover and
*decide on* local activities, family outings, day trips, vacations, restaurants,
parks, museums, events, and educational experiences. The goal is **not** endless
lists — it's helping a family make a **confident decision faster**.

## Who it serves
Starts with families (the "Founding Families" beta), then expands to accessibility
and special-needs travelers, young professionals, business travelers, single
adults, and baby boomers. Privacy-first, with child-safety considerations. Google
and Apple sign-in planned.

## The belief behind it
Families shouldn't have to spend hours researching how to spend meaningful time
together. Technology should reduce complexity, not create it — moving people from
information overload to confident action. **Plan Less. Explore More.**
