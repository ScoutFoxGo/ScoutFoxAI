# Scout Fox Go™ — Missing Modules Addendum (Scope of Work)

This addendum documents additional modules required for the complete Scout Fox
Go™ product, incorporated as binding deliverables.

## Required Modules

2.1 Scout Pay™ — Internal Payment Identity: wallet ledger, cashback logic, points redemption, fraud safeguards, UI dashboard.

2.2 Scout Points™ — Rewards Engine: point rules, redemption, leaderboards, AI-triggered bonuses, family pooling.

2.3 Notifications System: push, SMS, email alerts, weather alerts, reminders, smart card scheduling.

2.4 Family Collaboration System: multi-user trip editing, roles, guest access, shared lists and albums, analytics.

2.5 Scout Guide™: safety library, travel hacks, cultural notes, seasonal content, RAG integration.

2.6 Mood AI System: mood tagging, adaptive itineraries, mood smart cards, tone shaping, stress support.

2.7 RAG Knowledge Base: document ingestion, tagging, versioning, admin upload, vector indexing workflow.

2.8 Scout Scribe™ Full Trip Reports: photo highlights, captioning, story-style summary, themes, export history.

2.9 Administrative AI Tools: AI analytics dashboard, session viewer, feedback manager, trace logs, dataset admin.

2.10 Internationalization: Spanish, French, language toggle, multi-language RAG.

2.11 Accessibility Support: high contrast, large text, screen reader compatibility, low-cognitive-load mode.

2.12 Privacy & COPPA Safety: parental verification, minor profile restrictions, deletion rules, AI guardrails.

2.13 Optional Future: VR/AR + 3D Avatar (Phase 3 acknowledgement only).

2.14 Kickstarter & Marketing Tools: referral tracking, supporter badges, creator asset tools.

2.15 Smart Cards Automation Engine: weather, mood, rewards, seasonal cards with rule-based scheduling.

## Acceptance Criteria

Deliverables must meet functional, technical, UX, compliance, and performance
standards. Client may request changes if results do not meet module definitions.
